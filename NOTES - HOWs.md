## Neural network structure:


$$
\large
\text{Input Layer}
\rightarrow
\text{Hidden Layers}
\rightarrow
\text{Output Layer}
$$


### RECIPE
1. take some input neurons
2. take many  "hidden" layers of many neurons
3. take an output layer of some neurons
4. Formula:
   $\text{output}=\text{input }\cdot{\text{ weight }} + \text{ bias}$
5. Apply this to **each** neuron
